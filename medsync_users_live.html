Role Reference Guide
Descriptions, responsibilities, and access levels for each role
Admin
Full system access across all locations. Manages users, settings, and has visibility into portfolio-level analytics.
Access includes
All inventory & order screens
User management & role assignment
System settings & location setup
Portfolio analytics (all locations)
Receiving, cycle counts, goods lost
Manager
Multi-location operational access. Oversees day-to-day inventory, orders, and staff. Cannot modify system settings or add new locations.
Access includes
Inventory, orders, receiving
Cycle counts & goods lost
Analytics (assigned locations)
Create Receivers & Inventory Leads
No system settings or location creation
Inventory Lead
Single-location inventory specialist. Manages orders, receiving, and cycle counts. Limited analytics view with no user management.
Access includes
Inventory & purchase orders
Receiving & cycle counts
Goods lost reporting
Limited analytics (own location)
No user management
Receiver
Task-focused access for staff who receive deliveries and log goods lost. No inventory management or order screens.
Access includes
Receive deliveries
Submit goods lost reports
No inventory management
No purchase orders
No analytics or user management
