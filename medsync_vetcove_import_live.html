When to use this screen vs. regular Goods Lost
Regular Goods Lost is for items that were damaged, expired, or lost — no return was made and no credit is coming.

This screen is for Vetcove return credits only. When your team sends a product back to a vendor through Vetcove and Vetcove issues a credit invoice, that credit must be logged here so MedSync can offset the original goods lost entry and keep your records accurate.

How to use it:  In Vetcove, locate the credit invoice for the return → download it as a CSV or copy the line items → upload or paste it into this screen. MedSync will match each credit to the original goods lost record and document the offset.
