Clinic Staff Guide — Daily Inventory Operations
✓ Certified

This guide covers everything a clinic staff member needs to know to use MedSync day-to-day. MedSync keeps your inventory accurate, helps you catch discrepancies early, and syncs directly with Vetspire so you're never double-entering data.

🔗 Vetspire Integration: When you dispense a medication or supply in Vetspire, MedSync automatically decrements the quantity — no manual adjustment needed for dispensed items.
Inventory & Cycle Counts
1
Log in to MedSync
Go to medsync.vet and log in with the email and password provided by your admin. Your dashboard shows only your location's inventory.
2
Run your assigned cycle count
Navigate to Cycle Count in the sidebar. Your admin will assign which section (e.g., "Controlled Substances", "Surgical Supplies") to count. Scan or type quantities on hand.
⏱ Most cycle counts take 10–20 minutes
3
Flag variances
If your physical count differs from the system count, leave a note explaining the discrepancy (broken vial, damaged packaging, etc.). Do not adjust the count without a note.
4
Submit the count
Tap Submit Count. Your admin is notified automatically of any flagged variances. You'll see a green confirmation when it's received.
Receiving New Stock
1
Open the Receiving screen
Go to Receiving in the sidebar. Select the purchase order matching your delivery, or create a new receipt if no PO exists.
2
Enter quantities received
For each item, enter the quantity received and the lot number / expiration date from the packaging. Verify the unit of measure matches what's in the system.
3
Flag short shipments or damage
If a line item is shorted or received damaged, use the Flag Issue button. Your admin and the procurement team are notified immediately.
4
Confirm receipt
Tap Complete Receiving. Inventory is updated in real time and the purchase order is marked received.
✓ Inventory updates are immediate — no waiting for nightly syncs
Tips & Best Practices
✅ Count accuracy goal: 99%. Even small variances add up. When in doubt, count again before submitting.
⚠️ Never skip expiration dates when receiving. Lot lifecycle tracking depends on this data for recall and expiration alerts.
💡 Already dispensed in Vetspire? Don't manually adjust that item in MedSync — the Vetspire integration will handle it automatically within a few minutes.

Practice Admin Guide — Location & User Management
✓ Certified

As a Practice Admin (Super Admin), you manage users, locations, and billing for your organization. This guide walks through the key workflows you'll use regularly.

📍
Locations
Add & manage practice locations
👥
Users
Invite staff, set roles
📊
Analytics
Budget & spend reporting
💳
Billing
Plan & location limits
Adding a New Location
1
Go to New Location Setup
From the sidebar, tap New Location. You'll see your current plan details and available location slots.
2
Check your location limit
Your plan shows how many locations are included. If you're at 2 remaining slots, you'll see an upgrade prompt. Contact MedSync to discuss if you need more.
3
Fill in location details
Enter the location name, address, and primary contact. Assign it to a region (or create a new region). Add the Vetspire location ID if applicable.
4
Submit — MedSync activates it
Once submitted, MedSync provisions the location within one business day. You'll receive a confirmation email when it's active.
Inviting & Managing Users
1
Open Manage Users
Tap Manage Users in the sidebar. You'll see all users in your organization and their current roles.
2
Create a new user
Tap + New User. Enter their name, email, role (staff or manager), and assign their location(s). A welcome email is sent automatically with their login credentials.
3
Roles explained
Staff — can view inventory, run cycle counts, and receive stock for their assigned location.
Manager — all staff permissions plus procurement, analytics, and can add locations within plan limit.
Super Admin — full organizational access including user management and billing.
4
Deactivate a user
Click the user's name → Deactivate. Their session is immediately revoked and they cannot log back in. Their historical data is preserved.
Budget Analytics & Reporting
1
Access Analytics
Select Analytics from the sidebar. Choose a date range and filter by location or roll up to the full organization.
2
Review spend trends
The spend chart shows month-over-month drug and supply costs. Use the Supplier Breakdown tab to identify consolidation opportunities.
3
Quarterly reports
Open Quarterly Report for a one-page PDF-ready summary of inventory value, top spend categories, and cycle count compliance rates.
📥 Export as PDF for board or ownership reporting
Upgrading Your Plan
💳 Plan upgrades are handled through MedSync's secure Stripe checkout. Your new location limit is active immediately after payment.
1
Click "Upgrade MedSync Package"
The upgrade option appears in the New Location screen when you're near your plan limit, or you can request an upgrade through the banner that appears at 2 remaining slots.
2
Select your new tier
Review the available plans and location limits. Select the tier that fits your growth plans. You'll see your current plan highlighted.
3
Complete Stripe checkout
You're redirected to a secure Stripe checkout. Enter your payment details. Your card is charged the new monthly subscription immediately.
4
Return to MedSync — new limits are active
After payment, you're returned to the New Location screen with your updated slot count. No page refresh needed.
✓ Plan updates are instant — no waiting for manual approval

Platform Admin Guide — Provisioning & Support
✓ Certified

This section is for MedSync internal staff with medsync_superadmin access. It covers provisioning new customer organizations, managing billing records, and handling support escalations through the Platform Admin portal.

🔒 Restricted access. The Platform Admin portal is at medsync_platform_admin.html and requires a medsync_superadmin role. Never share login credentials. Each team member has their own account.
Provisioning a New Organization
1
Log in to Platform Admin
Navigate to medsync_platform_admin.html and log in with your MedSync superadmin credentials. You'll land on the Organizations dashboard.
2
Click "Provision New Organization"
Fill in the organization name, admin name, admin email, tier (plan), and Vetspire API token if provided. Review all fields before proceeding.
3
Step through the provisioning wizard
Step 1: Create Supabase auth account.
Step 2: Create public profile in users table.
Step 3: Create organizations record with tier and limit.
Step 4: Send welcome email with credentials (automatic).
✉️ Welcome email is sent automatically at step 4
4
Confirm and record
After provisioning, the organization appears in the org list. Verify the tier, location limit, and that the admin's role shows as admin. Log the provisioning event in the activity log.
Support Escalation Workflows
1
Billing issue — tier mismatch
In Platform Admin → select the organization → Billing tab. Review the change log. If the tier doesn't match what Stripe shows, use the Update Tier control to manually correct it, and note the reason.
2
User locked out
Go to the org → Users tab → find the user → Reset Password. This sends a Supabase password reset email directly to the user's inbox.
3
Suspending an organization
Use the Suspend Org toggle in Platform Admin only for non-payment or Terms of Service violations. Document the reason in the notes field. Suspension immediately revokes all user sessions for that org.
4
Feature flag overrides
The Feature Flags tab lets you toggle beta features per organization. Enable only features that are ready for the org's tier and technical setup. Document in Slack when enabling for a customer.
📞 Escalation path: Platform issues → admin@medsync.vet → Megan Henley. For Stripe billing disputes, all MedSync team members can access the Stripe dashboard.
