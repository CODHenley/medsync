Billing information
Contact and payment details for your MedSync subscription
Billed monthly on the 1st of each month. Plans starting mid-month are prorated based on days remaining in that month.
🔒 Billing info is locked. For security, billing details cannot be changed within 14 days of a previous update.
ACH Bank Transfer PRIMARY · REQUIRED
Monthly subscription payments are debited via ACH on the 1st of each month. Your bank details are stored securely and used only for MedSync subscription billing.
Credit Card BACKUP · REQUIRED
Required as a backup payment method. If an ACH payment fails or is returned, your card will be automatically charged within 24 hours. If both payment methods fail, a $35 late fee will be added to the following month's invoice. Visa, Mastercard, and Amex accepted.
